Bartosz Chaber

I am a postdoc at Warsaw University of Technology (Poland). My main area of expertise is writing fast numerical codes for plasma simulations using FDTD, FEM and PIC methods.

Most of my code is published either at my personal profile or as PLE²CS group.

In my free time I pretend to be a photographer.
